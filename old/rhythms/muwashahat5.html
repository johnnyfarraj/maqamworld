Recording Samples:

Muwashah Yamurru Ujuban in maqam Hijaz Kar Kurd (by Omar El Batch).

 Sittatu Ashar
 
32
4
             

     

Recording Samples:

Muwashah Badat Min Al Khidri (second section) in maqam Bayati.
 


Sittatu Ashar in Arabic means Sixteen. This rhythm is also called Sittatu Ashar Masri (from Masr - Egyptian).

 Warashan Arabi
 
32
4
             

     

Recording Samples:

Muwashah Qatili Bi Ghunji l-Kahali in maqam Bayati.
    Muwashah Ahu Min Jour Il Ghawani.
 


Warashan means Busy in Arabic, Arabi means Arab (Arabic).

 Shanbar Kabir

48
4
                 

         

 

Shanbar means hoop or something round/cyclic in Turkish (Cember). Kabir means Big or Large in Arabic. The Shanbar Kabir rhythm is also called Shanbar Mudaaf (Double Shanbar) or Jaftah.

See Also: Shanbar Halabi.
