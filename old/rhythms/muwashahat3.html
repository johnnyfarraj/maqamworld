Some Awis pieces start from the 3rd note (e.g. Muwashah Munyati Man...).

Recording Samples:

Muwashah Fika Kullu Ma Ara Hasan in maqam Bayati.
      Muwashah Adhkuru l-Hubba in maqam Nawa Athar (by Daoud Husni).
      Muwashah Nabbih El Nudman Sahi in maqam Hijaz Kar.
 


Mudawwar means Round or Circular in Arabic.

This Rhythm has many regional variations, all with a 12/4 signature.

Many Mudawwar pieces start from the 11th note (the last tak).
