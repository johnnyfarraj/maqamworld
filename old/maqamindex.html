Arabic Maqam Index


This section lists all maqams discussed in this website. This is by no means a comprehensive list of all Arabic maqams since many North African and Iraqi maqams are not included, instead this website covers the most commonly used Middle Eastern maqams. In practice, it's difficult to find a definitive list of Arabic maqams that all textbooks agree on, or a definitive reference on which maqams are strictly Arabic and which are Turkish or Persian.
