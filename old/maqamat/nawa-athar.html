Maqam Nawa Athar Family

 Maqam Nawa Athar

   
Hijaz on D
 
Nawa Athar on C
Hijaz on G

 


Notice that the first 3 notes of maqam Nawa Athar are a partial Nahawand tetrachord, which is often exploited during modulation to and from maqam Nahawand.

When starting on D, this maqam is called maqam Hisar.

 

Hear this Maqam:

Violin   Maqam Nawa Athar on C
Violin   Maqam Hisar on D
Oud   Maqam Nawa Athar on C
Buzuq   Maqam Nawa Athar on D

Recording Samples:

  Um Kulthum: Fakkaruni
  Doulab Nawa Athar
  Samai Jamil Aweis
  Farid El-Atrash: Gamil Gamal
  Muwashah Adhkurul Hubba
  Fawzi Sayeb: Taqsim Oud

 Maqam Athar Kurd (Kurdili Nawa Athar)

 
Athar Kurd on C
Hijaz on G

 

Maqam Athar Kurd is a variation on maqam Nawa Athar, with the 2nd note being 1/2 step lower. Notice that the first 3 notes are a partial Kurd tetrachord, which is often exploited to modulate to or from maqam Kurd.

  

Hear this Maqam:

Violin   Maqam Athar Kurd on C
Violin   Maqam Athar Kurd on D
Oud   Maqam Athar Kurd on C
Buzuq   Maqam Athar Kurd on C

Recording Samples:

 

Abdel Halim Hafez: Habeebaha
  Samai Kurd Simon Shaheen
  Taqsim Oud Riadh El Sunbati
  Samai Hijaz Kar Kurd Salah Al Mahdi
