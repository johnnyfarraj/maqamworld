Maqam Kurd Family

 Maqam Kurd
 
Ajam on F
  
Ajam on B
 
Kurd on D
Nahawand on G

Maqam Kurd starts with a Kurd tetrachord on the first note, and a Nahawand tetrachord on the 4th note (the dominant). The secondary ajnas are the Ajam trichord on the 3rd note, and another Ajam trichord on the 6th note. These are often used in modulation.

 Maqam Hijaz Kar Kurd

 
Hijaz on C
Hijaz on G

 

 
Kurd on C
Nahawand on F


Maqam Hijaz Kar Kurd has two forms shown above.The first form (Hijaz-Hijaz) is effectively maqam Hijaz Kar on C, and the second form (Kurd-Nahawand) is effectively maqam Kurd on C. Both forms should be used during the maqam's melodic development. The melody should start around the octave (upper C) and develop maqam Hijaz Kar, then descend from the octave by developing maqam Kurd back to the lower C. The classification of this maqam under the Kurd family is based on its ending.

Oud   Maqam Hijaz Kar Kurd on C
Buzuq   (Maqam Hijaz Kar Kurd on C
