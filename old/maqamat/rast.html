Maqam Rast Family

 Maqam Rast

 
Sikah on E
  
Rast on C
Rast on G

 
Sikah on E
  
Rast on C
Nahawand on G

Maqam Rast has two forms shown above. In general the first form (Rast-Rast) is used on the way up, and the second form (Rast-Nahawand) is used on the way down. The secondary jins is the Sikah trichord on the 3rd note, often used in modulation.
  

Hear this Maqam:

Violin   Maqam Rast on C
Oud   Maqam Rast on C
Oud   Maqam Rast on F
Buzuq   Maqam Rast on C
Buzuq   Maqam Rast on F

Samples:

  Doulab Rast (traditional)
  Muwashah Ya Shadi el-Alhan
  Muwashah Mala El-Kasat
  Muwashah Ya Udhayb Al-Marshaf
  Fairouz: Waraquh al-Asfar
  Ghannili Shwayy Shwayy
  Doulab Rast Leih Tilaweini
  Samai Rast Nikolaki
  Samai Rast George Michel

 

 Maqam Suznak (Suzinak)

 
Sikah on E
  
Rast on C
Hijaz on G

 


Maqam Suznak is very similar to maqam Rast, except the second tetrachord is Hijaz. Maqam Suznak is a very popular modulation for maqam Rast.
 

Hear this Maqam:

Violin   Maqam Suznak on C
Oud   Maqam Suznak on C
Buzuq   Maqam Suznak on C

Samples:

  Tahmilah Suznak
  Suznak Taqsim Qanun
  Ghannili Shway Shway

 Maqam Nairuz

 
Sikah on E
  
Rast on C
Bayati on G


Maqam Nairuz is very similar to maqam Rast, except the upper tetrachord is Bayati. Pieces in maqam Rast often modulate on maqam Nairuz and vice versa.

When starting on D, this maqam is called maqam Nishaburk.

 

Hear this Maqam:

Violin   Maqam Nairuz on C
Oud   Maqam Nairuz on
Buzuq   Maqam Nairuz on C
Buzuq   Maqam Nairuz on D

 Maqam Yakah (Yekah)

 
Rast on G
Rast on D

 


 
Rast on G
Nahawand on D

Maqam Yakah is a transpose of maqam Rast on lower G. Iin general the Rast-Rast form is used on the way up, and the Rast-Bayati and Rast-Nahawand forms are used on the was down. A third form is possible with Bayati as the upper jins.
  

Hear this Maqam:

Violin   Maqam Yakah on G
Oud   Maqam Yakah on G
Buzuq   Maqam Yakah on G

 Maqam Mahur

 
Rast on C
Ajam on G

Maqam Mahur is very similar to maqam Rast, except the upper tetrachord is Ajam.

  

Hear this Maqam:

Violin   Maqam Mahur on C
Oud   Maqam Mahur on C
Buzuq   Maqam Mahur on C
